About

I am a Computer Science graduate from the University of Southern Mindanao, with a Bachelor's degree in the field. When I was in my third year of college, I made the decision to learn frontend development. So, I took the initiative to learn independently by following roadmaps, reading documentation, and watching YouTube tutorials. I dedicated more than 10 hours a day to coding and improving my skills. As a result, I have now acquired decent knowledge in frontend development and am still learning some libraries and frameworks.

Currently, my primary focus is on building interactive, responsive, and elegant websites for both web and mobile platforms. I have a keen eye for details and always strive for pixel-perfect design. I maintain an unwavering curiosity and love for learning new things. I take great pride in delivering high-quality work and am committed to meeting deadlines. Despite my limited experience in real-world jobs in frontend development, I approach each challenge with confidence and believe that I can make valuable contributions to any team.
